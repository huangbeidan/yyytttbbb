                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
 at 2700 Cullen Boulevard in Pearland, Texas, as the ``Endy Nddiobong 
                    Ekpanya Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ENDY NDDIOBONG EKPANYA POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 2700 Cullen Boulevard in Pearland, Texas, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Endy Nddiobong Ekpanya Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Endy 
Nddiobong Ekpanya Post Office Building''.
